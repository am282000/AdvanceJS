What is buy sell stock ?

An array of stock prizes of a week is given like arr = [7,1,5,3,6,2] . A person can buy 1 stock and sell it to get the maximum profit

e.g. [7,1,5,3,6,2] if buy at 1 and sell at 6 => Output is 5 e.g. [7,6,5,4,3,2,1] if buy at 1 and sell at 1=> Output is 0
