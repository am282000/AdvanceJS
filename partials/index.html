What is partials ?

Splitting up a function into multiple smaller functions

Partials is same like curring just a small difference

In currying we call a function(a,b,c) => func(a)(b)(c)

like this i.e. always 1 aurguement at a time

In partials we call a function(a,b,c) => func(a)(b,c) or func (a,b)(c)

like this i.e. 1 arguement and after it can have remaining aurguementi.e.(more than one)
